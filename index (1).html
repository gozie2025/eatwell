Daily habits

Small shifts, lasting change

HABIT 01

Eat colour

Aim for 5 different colours across your meals — each pigment is a different set of plant nutrients.

HABIT 02

Slow down

It takes 20 minutes for your brain to register fullness. Put down your fork between bites.

HABIT 03

Read ingredients

If sugar, salt, or oil is in the first three ingredients, it's a treat — not a staple.

HABIT 04

Prep ahead

A batch of grains and roasted veg on Sunday makes healthy choices effortless all week.

HABIT 05

Crowd, don't cut

Add more good food rather than restricting. A full plate of nourishing food leaves little room for the rest.

HABIT 06

Sleep & eat well

Poor sleep raises hunger hormones. Protecting your sleep is one of the best nutrition decisions you can make.
